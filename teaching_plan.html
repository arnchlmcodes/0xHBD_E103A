6. Explanation (Level-Aware)
When we multiply fractions, we multiply the numerators together and the denominators together. For example, to multiply 3/2 and 4/5, we multiply the numerators (3 × 4) and the denominators (2 × 5). This gives us 12/10, which can be simplified to 6/5.
